Partners

PLEA e.V. (Göttingen)

P.L.E.A. e.V. is a non-profit, non-denominational association from Göttingen, which does not belong to any political party, which was founded in 2007 by African diaspora academics and former development workers. Our approach is to work together and on an equal footing with our partners in Africa to develop solution strategies for existing development problems. In cooperation with motivated development agencies in Africa, we want to promote incomplete and neglected development dynamics. Therefore, our development support covers all areas of life for which our support is requested and/or needed.

Migrationszentrum für Stadt und Lamkreis Göttingen

The Migration Center is a counseling, education and meeting center under the auspices of the Diakonieverband des Ev.-luth. Kirchenkreises Göttingen. The social and professional integration of migrants and the support of the self-help potential are the goals of our offers. Social pedagogues, lawyers, language course instructors, interpreters and an administrator make up our team. and an administrative assistant, supported by interns and volunteers. Our international team is committed to the humanitarian values of the Protestant Church. We work according to the integration plan of the state of Lower Saxony and the principles for Refugee Social Work of the Lutheran Church of Hanover.
