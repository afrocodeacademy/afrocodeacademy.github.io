About our Project

The project is aimed at introducing the possibilities and limitless potential of programming to the African community, specifically, black Africans living in Germany. We start at the grassroots by teaching mostly children the basics of computer programming through fun and games.

Free to Learn

The lessons are completely FREE and we plan to keep it this way. Our project is supported by our amazing volunteers with african heritage, who are fueled by their passion for programming and its importance to the African community. They see volunteering their time and resources to teach code as a way to give back to the African continent.

Why Join Afro Code Academy?

Our courses are taught by volunteers with PhDs and Masters degrees in Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science. They all have vast experiences in teaching and also developing software for some of the biggest companies across the world. We are always happy to have you as a volunteer.

What courses do we offer?

We have courses for 3 different age groups and these groups are sub-divided into smaller groups.

  • Ages 4 - 12 : We aim to introduce programming concepts and algorithmic thinking to kids. This will be done through programming of simple games and learning to perform basic computations through fun and games.

  • Ages 13 - 25 : These we expect to have some form of high school and university education but do not necessarily need to have a programming background. We introduce programming concepts and teach basics for data analysis and also software development.

  • Ages > 25 : These could be the working mum or dad, or people without any extensive university education. We aim to introduce them to frontend web design and basic programming concepts. The goal will be to get them to develop a website and possibly get them Freelance jobs if they are adequately motivated.
